Privacy Policy

Dementia Careblazers Behavior Tracker — March 2026

This app is provided by Dementia Careblazers as a free tool to help family caregivers track behaviors and prepare for medical appointments.

What we collect

  • Your email address, used only to create and access your account
  • Behavior logs, sleep logs, eating logs, mood check-ins, and medication notes that you choose to enter
  • The date and time of entries

How we use your data

  • Your personal entries are private to you and accessible only through your account
  • Your email address is never shared with or sold to third parties
  • Anonymized, aggregated data (e.g. most commonly logged behaviors across all users) may be used internally for content and educational purposes. This data cannot be traced back to you

Data security

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use Google Firebase, a secure cloud database, to store your information
  • Your email address is stored separately from your behavior data

Your rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support@careblazers.com
  • You can export your data using the app's built-in export features

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@careblazers.com
