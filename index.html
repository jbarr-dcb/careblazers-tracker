Medication list reminder

Bring: medication name, prescriber, dose, and any over-the-counter medications. Your doctor needs the full picture to avoid interactions.

📝

During the appointment

  • Write down the doctor's responses as they speak
  • Ask them to repeat or explain anything unclear — it's okay
  • You can say: "I want to make sure I understood — is this what you meant?"
  • If your loved one may become upset, hand your list to the nurse beforehand
👥

If your loved one gets upset at the appointment

  • Call the office ahead to explain the situation
  • Bring someone to sit with them while you speak privately
  • Ask if questions can be sent through the patient portal in advance
